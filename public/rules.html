Magic Chess Rules

Standard chess with selectable magic abilities.

Win / Draw

  • Win: Checkmate the opponent.
  • Draw: Stalemate, mutual agreement, or insufficient material (basic cases).
  • Timers: Shown in-game; no flag-loss enforced yet.
  • Advanced draws: 50-move and repetition tracking are not fully enforced yet.

Standard Moves

  • King: 1 step any direction; castling allowed if king/rook unmoved, path clear, not through check.
  • Queen: Any distance, straight or diagonal.
  • Rook: Any distance, straight.
  • Bishop: Any distance, diagonal.
  • Knight: L-shape (2+1), can jump over pieces.
  • Pawn: 1 forward; 2 forward on first move; capture diagonally; en passant supported; promotion on last rank.

Magic Abilities

  • Special Power (Pawns 2-step)Mode: Pawns may move two squares forward on any turn if the path is clear.
  • Ghost PawnMode: Each pawn once may ghost-walk over one piece straight ahead to the empty square behind; pawns may also 2-step anytime.
  • Heavy KnightMode: Landing stuns enemy pieces orthogonally adjacent for 1 turn; extended 3+1 vault can leap over two blockers.
  • Bishop SniperMode: Bishop may snipe (capture) up to 2 squares away on a diagonal without moving; adjacent friendlies are warded from capture.
  • Rook ChargeMode: On a long straight capture (3+ squares), the rook plows through and may advance one extra empty square if open.
  • Random Ability: Picks one of the above modes at start.

Special Move Support

  • Castling: Verified for unmoved king/rook, clear path, not through check.
  • En Passant: Supported immediately after a pawn 2-step.
  • Promotion: Choose piece on reaching last rank.
